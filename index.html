help YATZY! Rules Guide

YATZY! is a classic dice board game played with 5 dice. Your objective is to score the highest total by rolling combinations and filling out your scorecard.

Gameplay

  • A match consists of 13 rounds per player.
  • On your turn, you can roll the dice up to 3 times.
  • After the 1st or 2nd roll, tap any dice you want to hold (they won't be re-rolled).
  • Before your turn ends, you must select one empty scoring category to record your score.

Scoring Categories

Upper Section (Ones to Sixes)

  • Sum of all dice showing that number. For example, rolling ⚃ ⚃ ⚂ ⚄ ⚁ and scoring in Fours yields 8 points.
  • Upper Section Bonus: If the sum of your Upper Section scores is 63 or more, you receive a +35 bonus.
